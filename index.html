Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

3/4 cup browned butter (or melted if you're lazy)

1 cup brown sugar

1/3 cup cane sugar (i use demera)

1 egg + 1 egg yolk

vanilla extract, just use whatever you feel like

3/4 teaspoon salt

3/4 teaspoon baking soda

1 3/4 cups flour

1 cup chopped chocolate or chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Mix butter, brown sugar, cane sugar in a large bowl until one

2. Mix eggs and vanilla in the batter until combined

3. Add the rest of the ingredients and only mix until you see no streaks

4. Scoop the dough into balls and let the rest from 30 mins - overnight

5. Bake at 350F for 12-14 minutes and let them cool until you can pick them up

6. Enjoy!
